# World Happiness Report Dashboard (2015–2019)

## Tool
Power BI Desktop

## Dataset
World Happiness Report — 164 countries, 10 regions, 2015 to 2019

## What it shows
- Global average happiness score and key factor KPIs
- Economy impact on happiness score by region
- Health and happiness trend over 5 years
- Freedom and happiness trend over 5 years
- Trust vs happiness score by region
- Family support influence on happiness score

## Key Findings
- Family support is the highest contributing factor globally at 1.08
- Wealthier regions consistently score higher in happiness
- Health and happiness move together — both dipped in 2017 and recovered by 2019
- Regions with higher trust in government score significantly higher in happiness

## Skills Demonstrated
Data cleaning in Power Query, data visualisation, multi-factor analysis, trend analysis, regional comparison
